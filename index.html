Freight Shipping

We specialize in reliable and cost-effective East Africa shipping, including freight from China to Uganda, Tanzania, and South Sudan. With warehouse operations in China and a robust logistics network, we ensure your cargo is transported safely and efficiently.

Our Freight Services Include:

  • Air Freight: For time-sensitive cargo, offering effective delivery.
  • Ocean Freight: Full Container Load (FCL) and Less-than-Container Load (LCL) options.
  • Inland Transportation: Seamless delivery from ports to your final destination.
  • Real-time Tracking: Monitor your shipment's journey from start to finish.

Customs Clearance

Navigating customs can be complex. Our team of experts simplifies the process, handling all documentation, duties, and taxes to ensure your goods clear customs without delay.

Key Clearance Services:

  • Full documentation preparation and submission.
  • Accurate duty and tax calculation and payment.
  • Compliance with all East African import regulations.
  • Fast-track clearance for both air and sea shipments.

Reliable Product Sourcing

In addition to shipping, we support businesses with professional product sourcing in China. We assist clients in identifying verified suppliers, negotiating pricing, and coordinating safe transport to our warehouse.

Our Sourcing Support Includes:

  • Supplier identification and verification.
  • Price negotiation assistance.
  • Quality inspection coordination.
  • Supplier-to-warehouse logistics management.

Warehousing Solutions

Our secure, modern warehouse in Guangzhou serves as the central hub for your goods. We offer flexible storage options and expert consolidation services to optimize your shipments.

Warehouse Services:

  • Short & long-term storage.
  • Cargo inspection and labeling.
  • Consolidation of goods from multiple suppliers.
  • Professional packing and crating for export.

Logistics Management

We provide end-to-end logistics management, acting as your single point of contact for the entire supply chain. Our goal is to make your shipping process as simple and transparent as possible.

Our Management Covers:

  • Coordination with suppliers in China.
  • Booking freight and managing transit.
  • Handling all customs and port formalities.
  • Organizing final-mile delivery in East Africa.

Expert Consultation

Leverage our decade of experience in the China-East Africa trade lane. Our consultation services are designed to help you optimize your supply chain, reduce costs, and navigate market complexities.

We Advise On:

  • Import/export strategy and compliance.
  • Cost-saving opportunities in freight and duties.
  • Market entry analysis for East African countries.
  • Supplier negotiation and best practices.
